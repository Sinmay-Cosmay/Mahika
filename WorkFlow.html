Letters for Change

Letters Received-206

Work Flow

1. Encouraging young change-makers to write letters with a single prompt: "Write a letter on issues that you think will change your life."

2. Asking them to post their letters to us through WhatsApp on 9321654150.

3. Analysing their letters and delivering them to the right recipients.

4. Creating awareness about the most expressed concerns of children.

5. Posting and publishing a few letters on social media, in a small book every year.
