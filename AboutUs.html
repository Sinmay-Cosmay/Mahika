About Us

Letters for Change is a movement by the children, for the children. You may not be 18 yet. You may still be at school, but you are still aware of the issues that affect your life. And you want to talk about them. It may be about education, public transportation, environment, safety, and many other issues that affect all of our lives. Are you thinking about what one person or one letter can achieve? Mahatma Gandhi, Winston Churchill, Jawaharlal Nehru, Martin Luther King were all letter writers. And they brought change through the power of their words. The pen is indeed mightier than the sword.

Meet the Founder

Chief Letter Writer

Our passionate young founder, Mahika is a bright and charming 13-year-old who believes in the power of words, and more in the power of children. They can do anything, she says. Mahika is a fan of Marvel and the Jazz Age, and a proud Taurus. She is a master-chef in the kitchen and can bake the most delicious of pizzas from scratch. It is this magic of childhood, this energy, innocence and perspective that Letters For Change is all about. And our chief letter writer Mahika, is truly a Girl on Fire, for shes got her head in the clouds and she's not backing down.
